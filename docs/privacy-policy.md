# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 1, 2026**

Front Nine ("the App") is a golf course ranking app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## What We Collect

**Account Information**
When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email address (or Apple's private relay address if you choose to hide your email). We store your display name and the unique handle you create.

**Rankings & Activity**
The courses you rank, your ratings, and your ranking activity are stored to power the app's features. If your profile is public, other members can see your rankings.

**Location**
With your permission, the App accesses your location once to show nearby golf courses. We do not track or store your location.

## How We Use Your Data

- To display your rankings and profile to you and (if public) to other members
- To power social features: following, activity feeds, and ranking comparisons
- To search for golf courses near you

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Authentication** — manages sign-in securely
- **Cloud Firestore** — stores account data, rankings, and social connections
- **Apple MapKit** — powers course search (queries go directly to Apple)
- **GolfCourseAPI.com** — provides course statistics (par, slope, rating)

We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the app. This removes your profile, rankings, social connections, and activity data.
